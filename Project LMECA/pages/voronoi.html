Voronoi

On this canvas, you can visualize an animation of the Voronoi diagram. After triangulating the points, the seed points of the Voronoi Diagram are calculated by finding the intersection points of the medians of the edges of the triangulation. Subsequently, the edges of the Voronoi diagram are drawn based on the neighboring seed points.
